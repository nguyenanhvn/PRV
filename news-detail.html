The National Assembly of the Kingdom of Thailand approved the recommendation for the tourism industry to solve difficulties under the impact of the Covid-19 epidemic.The Nation Thailand cites The Minister of Finance, Lavaron Sangsnit said, “he suggested solutions to help Thailand tourism industry, which ware approved by The National Assembly of the Kingdom of Thailand

The solutions consist of giving more time to submit the filing of return of personal income tax from March to June. Businesses will have a tax abatement for local activities cost taking place from January 2020 to December 31, 2020 with 2 times deduction of the actual cost. Thailand's Government still decided to reduce aircraft fuel taxes for domestic flights from 4.726 bath/liter down to 0.2 bath/liter until September 30, 2020.

The Cabinet of Thailand approved plans for the State Bank of Thailand to provide soft loans and prolong a payback for tourism businesses affected by decreasing tourists in a current situation.
